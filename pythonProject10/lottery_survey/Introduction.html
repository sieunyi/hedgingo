Here's how the game works:

  1. Your Goal: Divide your money between two lotteries.
  2. About the Lotteries:
    • Lottery 1 and Lottery 2 are like a see-saw:
    • When one goes up, the other goes down.
    • This happens randomly each round.
  3. The Mechanics:
    • With a probability of p: Lottery 1's price decreases, Lottery 2's price increases.
    • With a probability of 1-p: Lottery 1's price increases, Lottery 2's price decreases.
  4. How to Play:
    • Use a simple slider to decide how much money to put in Lottery 1.
    • The remaining portion will automatically go to Lottery 2.
