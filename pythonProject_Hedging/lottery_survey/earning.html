Your potential outcome is calculated based on your choice and the probability of the events.
Here is a simple example to see how it is calculated:

Suppose you invest 50% in Investment A:

  • Investment A: Can give +10 or -5
  • Investment B: Can give +5 or -2
  • Probability of good outcome = 0.5 (50% chance)
Your potential outcome is calculated based on your choice and the probability of the events.

Bad Scenario (50% chance):
50% × (-5) + 50% × (+5) = -2.5 + 2.5 = 0

Good Scenario (50% chance):
50% × (+10) + 50% × (-2) = +5 - 1 = 4

Final Expected Outcome:
50% × (0) + 50% × (4) = 2
