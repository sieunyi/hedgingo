Using the example we have seen so far:
  • Apple: Can give +10 or -10
  • Banana: Can give +10 or -10
  • Chances of one event (p) = 0.5 (50% chance)
Potential outcome is calculated based on your choice.

Suppose you invest 80% in Apple and 20% in Banana.

With 50% chance:
0.8 × Apple poor (-10) + 0.2 × Banana well (10) = -6
With 50% chance:
0.8 × Apple well (10) + 0.2 × Banana poor (-10) = 6

Your calculated potential outcome is:
50% × (-6) + 50% × (6) = 0
