This chart displays the chances of the two events.

'100-p' is the chance of:
• Apple performing well (+)
• Banana performing poor (-)

'p' is the chance of:
• Apple performing poor (-)
• Banana performing well (+)

This chance is used to calculate the expected outcome of the lottery.

For instance, Apple's expected outcome is:

50% × (-10) + 50% × 10 = 0

Banana's expected outcome is:

50% × (10) + 50% × (-10) = 0

Important: These chances change over rounds.
